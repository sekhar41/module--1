india v/s australia

The Australia Tour of India 2022 is an international Bilateral series, also the part of Future Tour Programs (FTP) announced by International Cricket Council in 2009. India will host the Bilateral series of Australia Tour of India 2022, beginning on September 20, 2022. The 1st T20 match between India vs Australia will be played at Punjab C.A. Stadium, Mohali on Sep 20, 2022 at 19:00 local time onwards.
